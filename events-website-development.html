OpenInvite: Events Website

OpenInvite was the first start-up I built during University while taking part in the Queen’s Innovation Summer Institute - a 4 months entrepreneurial bootcamp. We won a $10 K award plus a $60 K grant to continue the venture. I self-taught design and front-end development. I learned what it takes to work with a developer and freelance contractors and how to build an MVP.

OpenInvite was an events website that made it easy to find free, local events and full-day, multi-day outdoor activities. We negotiated a 15 - 20 % cut from merchants in the Kingston area and brought groups of students on activities (such as paint balling or stand up paddle boarding), making a few thousand dollars.
